Sectors trading is used by traders who want to gain exposure to a specific area of the economy, for example the oil and gas sector. With sectors trading, you can get exposure to a basket of related stocks – from the same index and industry – with a single trade. This enables you to go long or short on the sector as a whole without buying and selling companies’ individual shares. (IG, n.d.)
